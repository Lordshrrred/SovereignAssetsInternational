# Global Mercury Recovery Brand Asset Kit

Transparent PNG and SVG exports generated from the supplied logo photo.

## Included
- Full-color horizontal logo
- White horizontal logo
- Black horizontal logo
- Icon-only full-color, white, and black
- Stacked full-color, white, and black logos
- Favicons at 16x16, 32x32, 48x48, and 180x180, plus favicon.ico

## Notes
- PNG files are high-resolution transparent exports.
- SVG files are traced vector exports with transparent backgrounds.
